We welcome you to Casa Malola 30D. This is our little piece of paradise in the sun! We hope you agree!

Situated on the prestigious Casas del Sol development, in the heart of Playa Blanca, it is just walking distance to the town, beaches, shops, restaurants and bars. Our bungalow is located towards the back of the complex and away for any road noise and is suited for both young and older guests due to the close proximity to everything you need for a stress free holiday.

Swimming Pool

The solar heated salt water communal swimming and baby pool with resident Lifeguard, is on site and just a couple of minutes walk from Casa Malola. Plenty of sun beds and parasols around the pool. Ice creams and drinks can be purchased from Reception.

Tennis courts and Boules can be played on site and also booked at Reception.

Accomodation

Casa Malola

30D sleeps 4 with two bright bedrooms, both with ceiling fans for those hot sticky nights and mosquito nets at the windows.

Kitchen

The kitchen includes a full size halogen hob cooker, microwave, fridge and dishwasher. Plenty of crockery and cutlery, glasses, mugs and tea cups.

Patio

The patio is south facing and enjoys plenty of sun most of the day. For alfresco dining there is a BBQ, table and rattan chairs. A parasol and sun lounger and ceiling fan to cool you down!

Bedroom 1

Bedroom 1 has a super king size double bed, wardrobes, bedside cabinets, reading lights with mosquito nets at the window and ceiling fan to keep you cool during hot sticky nights.

Bedroom 2

Bedroom 2 has twin beds, wardrobe, chest of drawers. with mosquito nets at the window and ceiling fan to keep you cool during hot sticky nights.

Living Area

The airy lounge has a sofa and dining table and chairs. The large TV has an abundance of English channel’s , including sports, movies news and much more.
